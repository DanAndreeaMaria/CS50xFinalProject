Let's track your daily nutrients!

Stay in control of your nutrition, one food at a time. Build better habits with every log.

Fruits are rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants. They support immune health, skin vitality, and digestion. Naturally sweet and hydrating, they're a great source of quick energy and essential nutrients like vitamin C and potassium.

Vegetables are packed with fiber, minerals, and phytonutrients. Low in calories but high in volume, they promote fullness, gut health, and disease prevention. Leafy greens, cruciferous veggies, and colorful varieties offer the greatest benefits.

Legumes — like beans, lentils, and chickpeas — are excellent plant-based protein sources. They're rich in fiber, iron, and complex carbs, making them ideal for sustained energy, heart health, and blood sugar control.

Nuts are energy-dense foods loaded with healthy fats, protein, and antioxidants. Just a small handful can boost brain function, heart health, and satiety. Popular varieties include almonds, walnuts, and cashews.

Whole grains are nutrient-packed staples rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. They support digestion, heart health, and steady energy. Common examples include oats, brown rice, quinoa, and whole wheat.

Seeds such as chia, flax, and pumpkin, are nutritional powerhouses. They deliver omega-3s, protein, fiber, and essential minerals like zinc and magnesium. They support metabolism, inflammation control, and overall wellness.
